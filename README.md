## Why this matters

Of the billion people with mental-health issues, only 1 in 14 ever does anything about it. The other 13 scroll, vent, push through.

Bemellou is built for those 13 — the people who won't book therapy, won't download Calm, won't admit they need anything. The plush is the soft entry point. The app is what's behind the door.

We're not therapy. We're not crisis care. We're a community-rooted wellness brand built on the belief that *you were never broken to begin with*.

## What we ship

A small team using AI agents as multipliers. Six in-production internal tools across acquisition, research, ops, and the product itself. Most code lives in private repos — strategy is part of our edge — but the surfaces below describe what each one does.

### 🧠 bemellou-engine *(internal)*
Acquisition + research engine. Monitors mental-health-adjacent communities at scale. Drafts personalized replies behind a manual approval gate. Aggregates topic signal into market research and a hook bank for content.

### 🎯 bemellou-funding-bot *(internal)*
Continuously surfaces accelerators, fellowships, grants, and competitions for early-stage consumer wellness. 29+ programs tracked.

### 📺 [bemellou-youtube-finder](https://github.com/bemellou/bemellou-youtube-finder)
YouTube creator discovery + qualification. Built on the engagement-scoring model that drove $3M+ at Black Forest Supplements.

### 🎓 [bemellou-uni-pipeline](https://github.com/bemellou/bemellou-uni-pipeline)
University partnership tracker. Active conversations with Northwestern, UC Berkeley, and BU.

### 🎛 [bemellou-command-center](https://github.com/bemellou/bemellou-command-center)
Internal ops dashboard. Single pane of glass for the team — Shopify, ad platforms, GA4, Stripe.

### 📱 bemellou-app *(internal)*
The product. Live at [chubiez.app](https://chubiez.app) during pre-launch iteration. React + TypeScript + Vite, wrapped with Capacitor for iOS + Android. Free tier (with the plush) + three paid tiers, adaptive course flows, community, live psychologist sessions.

## How we build

The CEO writes specifications; AI agents ship code. The internal tools above were each built by one person directing one or more coding agents (Claude Code, Cursor, OpenHands). The app — three weeks, zero prior coding experience, after firing the SaaS firm originally hired.

Strategy stays internal. What we share publicly is what we *are*, not how we get there.
